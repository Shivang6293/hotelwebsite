About Hotel CPI

Delight in intuitive service, comfort and a personalised touch; qualities you will only find at Hotel Central Point International, where we await you with a warm welcome. It is a luxurious hotel with three star amenities. Our infrastructure and the variety of services are ever multiplying with the change in life style of the people and the demand of the hotel and tourism industry.

Our hotel is facilitated with well-furnished rooms, ample space for car parking, safe deposit lockers, cloak room facilities, Doctor on call, excellent restaurant with different cuisine operating 24x7 throughout the year. Our hotel reflects a relaxing ambiance, a comfort zone in every corner and is designed in a manner that is suitable for both business and pleasure-stay purpose.
